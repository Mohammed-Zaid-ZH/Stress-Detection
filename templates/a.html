Stay Stress Free

We are a passionate team of AI enthusiast dedicated to leveraging the power of artificial intelligence to promote mental well-being. Our goal is to create technological solutions that empower individuals and organizations to better manage stress, improve productivity, and foster mental resilience. In an increasingly fast-paced world where stress levels are on the rise, we believe that technology has the potential to revolutionize how we understand, detect, and manage mental health issues, particularly stress.

Stress is a natural human response to challenges. It has become a widespread issue impacting both mental and physical health. Prolonged stress can lead to conditions like anxiety, depression, cardiovascular diseases, and weak immune system. In fast-paced workplaces, high stress often arises from demanding workloads and tight deadlines. Chronic patients require constant stress monitoring to prevent health deterioration and improve treatment outcomes. Traditional stress assessment methods, like self-reporting and invasive monitoring, are subjective and impractical for continuous use, highlighting the need for automated, non-invasive stress detection systems.

To tackle this, our system provides an advanced stress detection interface where the system will analyse user's facial expressions to identify stress levels using deep learning models. This functionality is facilitated through non-intrusive methods such as cameras to monitor visual cues and the system captures real-time data without physical contact, ensuring a seamless experience. Utilizing techniques like CNNs and WMDNNs, our approach focuses on accurately detecting stress through facial analysis, providing valuable insights into mental well-being.
